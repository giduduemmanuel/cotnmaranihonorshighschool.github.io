About Us

School Background

COTN Marani Honors High School is a christian founded secondary school registered under the ministry of education and sports.

We are a day and boarding secondary school, with O and A level, located in Lira city west division.

We are an institution that offers value driven education, promoting spiritual growth,

moral discernment, and social responsibility empowering students

to make positive impact in communities

We are a school that is committed to providing quality education,

and we are dedicated to the holistic development of our students.
